"Fear's not a bad thing. It's quite a useful thing in the right circumstances"(Fletcher 60)

This quote about fear resontates with me and seems very relevant right now as many people are scared of things and also don't like the idea of being scared. This quote and the context around it explain that it could be very helpful to help yourself and others and keep them safe.

I chose the book A Boy and His Dog at the End of the World because it seemed to have a good concept. The perceived concept of the book that I chose was the idea that the book was based on a young kid that was seeking revenge for his dog. While, this book did follow the idea of revenge, it also mentioned alot of other themes such as one trusting others.
